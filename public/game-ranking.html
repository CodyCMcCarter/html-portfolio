The Best Video Games According to Cody

3 Games I'll Always Remember.

Picking favorites is hard, so here are 3 games from different points in my life that I remember to this day.

Vectorman

A game from my childhood that I never forgot. A fun 2D platformer/shooter for the Sega Genesis.

Halo 2

My first modern FPS and a true classic. I still remember LAN parties with friends.

Ghost of Tsushima

Just a fantastic game all around. Super solid singleplayer and very fun multiplayer that was added later for free. I love my horse.
